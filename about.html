Our History

Founded in 1990, ModernCare Hospital has been at the forefront of medical innovation and patient care. What started as a small community hospital has grown into a world-class medical facility serving patients from around the globe.

Over the years, we have continuously expanded our services and facilities to meet the growing healthcare needs of our community while maintaining our commitment to excellence and compassionate care.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional healthcare services with compassion, innovation, and excellence, making a positive difference in the lives of our patients and their families.

Our Vision

To be the leading healthcare provider, recognized globally for our commitment to patient care, medical innovation, and community well-being.
